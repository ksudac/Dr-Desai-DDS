Dental Resources

The websites listed below contain interesting and helpful dental information. They are created by the Academy of General Dentistry and the American Dental Association to provide consumers with dental information and promote healthy oral care. If you have any questions about the any information in the links, don't hesitate to contact us.



www.mouthhealthy.org - American Dental Association

Informative, fun and easy to use website from the ADA. Contains a wide variety of dental health information. The site is sorted by age group so you can focus on dental concerns within your age group.


www.knowyourteeth.com - Academy of General Dentistry

Dental care and oral health information from the Academy of General Dentistry. This site is designed by the AGD to meet the needs of consumers who have questions and concerns about their dental care and oral health.


Dictionary of Dental terms - American Dental Association

Simple and useful glossory of terms to help you understand dental jargon.
